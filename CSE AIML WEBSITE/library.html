LIBRARY



Facilities provided by the library are as follows:

1.Infrastructure:
State of-the-art Library to provide the right academic support with reading hall facility.The seats and tables are designed in a way that you can concentrate long hours and focus on academics.
2.Wi-Fi Facility:
The entire library is Wi-Fi enabled; members can use their configuration laptops to access electronic resources subscribed by the library.
3.Service:
The magazines and journals are made available to library users. students have to read in library only.
4.Book Lending Service:
This service is offered to all library users. The resources borrowed from the library shall be returned on or before the due date during library working hours. Its terms and conditions are mentioned in Library Rules and Regulations.
5.Open Access:
It provides open access to all library users. They can browse, read the any references in the reference section. It helps users to make full use of the resources available in the library.
6.Loss and Defacement of Books, Journals, and other Library Articles:
The cost of replacement with additional 20% handling fee will be charged to the person for loss or damage to the library materials. Book grants may not be used to settle library dues. Any member if determined to have defaced or damaged any library item intentionally will have his/her library privileges revoked for a period of time determined by the library committee. Library privileges may be revoked permanently for multiple or serious offences.
7.Number of books:
Library with more than 73,195 Books, 43,451 e-Journals and 8,276 e-Books and all are course related which can help them in understanding the subjects better. It also have 80 National and 81 International Journals Subscription.
8.Photocopying:
A photocopy machine will be available in the library all the times for use of the faculty and library staff. All other users have to avail the common photocopy facility. Photocopy privileges may be extended in case non-availability of the common photocopy facility and is determined on by the librarian.
