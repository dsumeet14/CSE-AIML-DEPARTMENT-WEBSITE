1. Mismatch Day:
Cultural Fest started with mismatched day. Mismatched day simply means to mismatch clothes to put them together although they do not go together well. During Mismatched day everyone was looking oddly beautiful. Various games were also played by everyone.
2. Cultural Day:
Second day of cultural fest started with cultural day. Everyone was looking great in ethinic and traditional wear. We even had an after DJ party when the cultural day ended.
3. Sports Day:
There were variety of sports played during sports days like football, kho-kho, kabaddi, shotput, long-jump, high-jump, 100m race, 200m race, etc.Most of the students participated and gave their best performance.
