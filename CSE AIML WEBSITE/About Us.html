About Us

Department of Engineering and Technology

B.Tech Computer Science Engineering (AI-ML) is a 4-years undergraduate course that is divided into 8 semesters. The course is designed to make the students professionally and technically sound in advanced learning systems that are based on algorithms of artificial intelligence. The course develops the skills of creating fastest working apps or solutions integrated with analytical information ensuring 100% accuracy in the output.

Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed to be University(Department of Engineering And Technology) is the first institute having AI-ML batch all over India as it the fast-evolving field and still new in India. Our institute provides advanced labs with well equipped technologies for better understanding of syllabus

The course imparts the detailed knowledge of the programming languages that are used in developing artificial intelligence and machine learning softwares and also devices.B.Tech/BE Computer Science Engineering (AI-ML)is huge. Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) is bound to revolutionise the future tech industry. The AI and ML are now at a nascent stage and it is set to create a shift in the tech industry. It is estimated that by 2025 the demand for AI and ML skilled professionals increases by 40% and the tech giants will adapt this technology for their daily business. Now the industry is looking for skilled AI and ML professionals and are ready to pay a handsome salary. The interested students can join masters by joining M.Tech/ME or MS programmes in AI and ML.
